E-commerce 2013

By Laudon & Traver

Overview

E-commerce 2013 offers a comprehensive exploration of the digital marketplace, focusing on business models and technological infrastructure. Laudon and Traver analyze the impact of social media and mobile platforms on contemporary retail strategies. The text provides real-world cases that illustrate the challenges and opportunities in the rapidly evolving world of online commerce.
