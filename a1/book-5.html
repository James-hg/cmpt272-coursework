The Economic Way of Thinking

By Heyne et al.

Overview

This text introduces the principles of microeconomics through the lens of individual choice and market coordination. Heyne and his co-authors emphasize how economic reasoning can be applied to everyday decision-making and social interactions. It is designed to help students understand the underlying logic of market processes and public policy consequences.
