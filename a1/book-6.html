Database Processing

By Kroenke & Auer

Overview

Kroenke and Auer provide a thorough introduction to database design, application development, and administration. The book explores the relational model and SQL while also addressing modern NoSQL and Big Data requirements. It is an essential guide for ensuring data integrity and efficient information management in contemporary enterprise environments.
