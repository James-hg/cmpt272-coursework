Global Marketing Management

By Keegan

Overview

Keegan provides a comprehensive analysis of global marketing strategies in a rapidly integrating world economy. The text focuses on the environmental and cultural factors that shape international business decisions and market entry. Students will explore practical frameworks for managing marketing mix elements across diverse geographic and economic landscapes.
